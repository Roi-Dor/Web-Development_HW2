Berlin Travel Guide

Berlin, the vibrant capital of Germany, is a city that seamlessly blends rich history with modern charm. From world-class museums to thriving art scenes and legendary nightlife, Berlin has something for every kind of traveler.

Brandenburg Gate

An iconic symbol of Berlin and one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world, the Brandenburg Gate is a must-visit attraction.

Opening Hours: 24/7, Price: Free
