Paris

Discover the city of love and lights, where art, fashion, and cuisine come together in perfect harmony. From the iconic Eiffel Tower to the tranquil Seine River, Paris is a city that will leave you enchanted and inspired.

Eiffel Tower

The most iconic landmark in Paris, the Eiffel Tower offers breathtaking views of the city from its top deck.

Opening hours: 9:30 AM - 11:45 PM, Price: €17 - €29

Louvre Museum

One of the world's largest and most famous museums, the Louvre is home to an impressive collection of art and artifacts from around the world.

Opening hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Price: €18
