London Travel Guide

Explore the vibrant city of London, steeped in history, culture, and entertainment. From iconic landmarks to world-class museums, London has something for everyone.

The British Museum

One of the world's greatest museums, with a collection of over 8 million objects.

Opening hours: 10am - 5:30pm, Price: Free

The London Eye

A giant Ferris wheel offering stunning views of the city.

Opening hours: 10am - 8:30pm, Price: £27 - £37

Buckingham Palace

The official residence of the British monarch, open to visitors during the summer months.

Opening hours: 9:30am - 6:30pm, Price: £25 - £30
