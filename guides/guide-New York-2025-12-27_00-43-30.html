New York Travel Guide

Experience the city that never sleeps, from iconic skyscrapers to diverse neighborhoods and world-class museums.

Statue of Liberty

An iconic symbol of freedom and democracy, the Statue of Liberty is a must-visit attraction in New York.

Opening hours: 9:30 AM - 4:15 PM, Price: $21.50 - $31.50

Central Park

An 843-acre green oasis in the middle of Manhattan, Central Park is perfect for walking, jogging, or picnicking.

Opening hours: 6:00 AM - 1:00 AM, Price: Free

The Metropolitan Museum of Art

One of the world's largest and most renowned museums, the Met features a vast collection of art and artifacts from around the world.

Opening hours: 10:00 AM - 5:30 PM, Price: $25 - $30
