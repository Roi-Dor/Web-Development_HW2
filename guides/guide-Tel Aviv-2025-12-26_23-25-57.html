Tel Aviv Travel Guide

Experience the vibrant city of Tel Aviv, where stunning beaches meet a thriving nightlife, and historic landmarks blend with modern architecture.

White City

Explore the UNESCO-listed White City, featuring over 4,000 Bauhaus-style buildings.

Opening hours: 24/7, Price: Free

Carmel%20Market

Visit the bustling Carmel Market, offering a wide range of fresh produce, spices, and souvenirs.

Opening hours: 8am-7pm, Price: Free

Jaffa%20Port

Discover the historic Jaffa Port, featuring stunning views, restaurants, and shops.

Opening hours: 24/7, Price: Free
