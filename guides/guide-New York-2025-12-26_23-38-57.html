New York Travel Guide

Experience the city that never sleeps, from iconic skyscrapers to world-class museums and diverse culinary scenes.

Statue of Liberty

An iconic symbol of freedom and democracy, the Statue of Liberty is a must-visit attraction in New York City.

Open daily from 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM, tickets start at $21.50.

Central Park

An 843-acre green oasis in the middle of Manhattan, Central Park offers a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Open daily from 6:00 AM to 1:00 AM, free admission.

Metropolitan Museum of Art

One of the world's largest and most renowned museums, the Met features a vast collection of art and artifacts from around the world.

Open Sunday to Thursday from 10:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Friday and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM, tickets start at $25.
