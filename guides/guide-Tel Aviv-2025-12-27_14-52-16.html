Tel Aviv Travel Guide

Tel Aviv, a vibrant city on the Mediterranean coast, offers a unique blend of culture, history, and entertainment. From beautiful beaches to world-class museums, Tel Aviv has something for everyone.

Top 3 Attractions

White City

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the White City is a collection of over 4,000 Bauhaus-style buildings.

Opening hours: 24/7, Price: Free

Tel%20Aviv%20Museum%20of%20Art

One of Israel's leading art museums, featuring a collection of modern and contemporary art.

Opening hours: Mon, Wed, Sat: 10am-6pm, Tue, Thu: 10am-9pm, Fri: 10am-2pm, Price: 50 ILS

Carmel%20Market

A bustling marketplace offering a wide range of fresh produce, souvenirs, and street food.

Opening hours: Sun-Thu: 8am-7pm, Fri: 8am-2pm, Price: Free
