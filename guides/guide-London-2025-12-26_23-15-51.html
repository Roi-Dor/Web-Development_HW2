London Travel Guide

Discover the vibrant city of London, filled with iconic landmarks, world-class museums, and a diverse cultural scene.

Buckingham Palace

The official residence of the British monarch, Buckingham Palace is a must-visit attraction in London.

Opening hours: 9:30 AM - 3:30 PM, Price: £25-£30

The British Museum

One of the world's greatest museums, the British Museum houses a vast collection of artifacts from ancient civilizations.

Opening hours: 10:00 AM - 5:30 PM, Price: Free

The London Eye

For a panoramic view of the city, take a ride on the London Eye, a giant Ferris wheel located on the South Bank of the Thames.

Opening hours: 10:00 AM - 8:30 PM, Price: £27-£37
