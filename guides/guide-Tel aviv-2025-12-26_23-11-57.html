Tel Aviv Travel Guide

Introduction

Tel Aviv, the vibrant city that never sleeps, is a melting pot of culture, history, and entertainment. From its stunning beaches to its world-class museums, Tel Aviv has something for everyone.

Top 3 Attractions

White%20City

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the White City is a collection of over 4,000 buildings built in the 1930s in the International Style.

Practical Info: Open 24/7, free admission

Carmel%20Market

One of the largest and most famous markets in Israel, the Carmel Market offers a wide range of fresh produce, spices, and souvenirs.

Practical Info: Open Sunday to Friday, 7am-7pm, free admission

Tel%20Aviv%20Beach

With its pristine waters and golden sand, the Tel Aviv Beach is the perfect spot to relax and soak up the sun.

Practical Info: Open 24/7, free admission
