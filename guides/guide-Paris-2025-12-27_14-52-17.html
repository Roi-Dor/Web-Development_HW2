Paris Travel Guide

Discover the City of Light, famous for its stunning architecture, art museums, and romantic atmosphere. From the iconic Eiffel Tower to the charming streets of Montmartre, Paris has something to offer for every kind of traveler.

Eiffel Tower

The most iconic landmark in Paris, the Eiffel Tower offers breathtaking views of the city from its top level.

Opening hours: 9:30 AM - 11:45 PM, Price: €17 ( elevator to top), €7 (staircase to 2nd floor)

Louvre Museum

One of the world's largest and most famous museums, the Louvre is home to an impressive collection of art and artifacts from ancient civilizations to the 21st century.

Opening hours: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM, Price: €18 (permanent collections), free admission on the first Sunday of each month

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A beautiful and historic Catholic cathedral that was built in the 12th century, famous for its stunning architecture and stunning rose windows.

Opening hours: 8:00 AM - 6:45 PM, Price: free admission, but guided tours available for a fee
